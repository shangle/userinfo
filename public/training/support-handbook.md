# UserInfo Support Handbook: Onboarding Guide

## Overview
UserInfo is a client-side diagnostic tool that automates the collection of browser, OS, and network information. This guide is designed for support staff to help them use UserInfo to resolve customer issues faster.

## Quick Start
1. Send the customer the UserInfo link: `https://shangle.github.io/userinfo/`
2. Ask them to read the **Summary Card** (Browser, OS, IP).
3. If more detail is needed, ask them to click **'Copy details'** and paste it into your support chat or email.

## Key Modules
- **Summary:** Quick overview of the customer's environment.
- **Actions:** One-button email or copy-to-clipboard for the customer.
- **Help Guide:** Tailored troubleshooting steps based on the customer's environment.
- **Common Problems:** Information on VPNs, Tor, and Cookies.
- **Technical Details:** Deep diagnostics (screen resolution, hardware info).
- **Link Generator:** For support staff to pre-fill links with their email and subject.

## Best Practices
- **Pre-fill links:** Use the Link Generator to create a custom URL for the customer with your email address already included.
- **Guide the customer:** "I've sent you a link to our diagnostic tool. Please open it and click the 'Open email' button to send us your details."
- **Privacy First:** Remind customers that no data is collected automatically; they choose when and how to share it.

## FAQ for Support
- **Q: Does this tool work on mobile?**
  A: Yes! UserInfo is fully responsive and detects mobile browser/OS details accurately.
- **Q: Is any personal data stored?**
  A: No. UserInfo is a client-side tool. Data is only transferred if the user explicitly sends an email or copies and pastes information.
- **Q: What if the customer is offline?**
  A: UserInfo will still work and show local details (Browser, OS), but network-dependent info (IP Address, ISP) will show as unavailable.
