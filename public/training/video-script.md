# UserInfo Onboarding: Support Team Video Script

## Introduction (0:00 - 0:45)
**Visual:** Screen recording of the UserInfo landing page.
**Audio:** "Welcome to UserInfo! This is a professional diagnostic tool designed to help you, our support team, gather technical details from customers quickly and securely. No more asking 'what browser are you using?' or 'what version of Windows do you have?'. UserInfo does it all automatically."

## The Customer Experience (0:45 - 2:00)
**Visual:** Customer view of the diagnostic dashboard.
**Audio:** "When a customer opens the UserInfo link, they see a clean, non-intimidating dashboard. The most important information—browser, OS, and IP address—is front and center. They can simply read this to you, or click 'Copy details' to send you a full report."

## Using the Link Generator (2:00 - 3:30)
**Visual:** Scrolling down to the 'Create a support link' card.
**Audio:** "To make things even easier, you can use the Link Generator. Type in your support email and a subject line, then copy the generated link. When the customer opens this link, their 'Send' button will already be pre-filled with your address. They just have to click 'Open email' and hit send."

## The "Help" and "Common Problems" Sections (3:30 - 4:45)
**Visual:** Clicking on a help topic and the VPN/Tor section.
**Audio:** "If a customer is having trouble with specific issues like VPNs or cookies, point them to the 'Help' and 'Common Problems' cards. These sections provide tailored advice based on their environment, helping them solve simple issues without your direct intervention."

## Advanced Technical Details (4:45 - 6:00)
**Visual:** Expanding the 'Technical details' section.
**Audio:** "For complex bugs, expand the 'Technical details' section. This includes data like screen resolution, viewport size, and hardware concurrency. This information is invaluable for our engineering team to reproduce and fix issues."

## Conclusion (6:00 - 7:00)
**Visual:** UserInfo logo and support resources link.
**Audio:** "UserInfo is here to reduce your resolution time and make support interactions smoother for everyone. For more details, check out the Support Handbook in the training resources. Thanks for watching, and let's make support better with UserInfo!"
